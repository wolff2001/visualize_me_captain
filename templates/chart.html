Amount of carbon dioxide emission by year

State's Co2 emissions include CO2 emissions from direct fuel use across all sectors, including residential, commercial, industrial, and transportation, as well as primary fuels consumed for electricity generation.

States have attempted to build infrastructure to facilitate the adoption of electric vehicles. This can help reduce transportation related emissions. By building charging stations, a state can help facilitate individuals to purchase electric vehicles as well as potentially helping companies consider electric vehicles as their main source of commercial transportation.
